Security

Vulnerability Disclosure

Last updated: May 25, 2026

We take the security of Cynda and our users' data seriously. If you discover a security vulnerability, we encourage you to report it responsibly so we can address it promptly.

How to Report

Send a description of the vulnerability to support@cynda.tech with the subject line Security Vulnerability Report.

Please include:

  • A clear description of the vulnerability and its potential impact.
  • Steps to reproduce the issue or a proof-of-concept.
  • Any relevant screenshots, logs, or supporting material.

Our Commitment

  • We will acknowledge your report within 48 hours.
  • We will investigate and keep you informed of our progress.
  • We aim to resolve confirmed vulnerabilities within 30 days.
  • We will not take legal action against researchers who report in good faith.

Responsible Disclosure

We ask that you:

  • Do not publicly disclose the issue until we have had a chance to address it.
  • Do not access, modify, or delete data belonging to other users.
  • Do not perform actions that could disrupt the service for others.

Scope

This policy applies to the Cynda web application and API hosted at cynda.chat.

Out of scope: third-party services we integrate with (Slack, Google BigQuery, OpenRouter). Please report vulnerabilities in those services directly to their respective security teams.

Contact

For all security-related reports, email support@cynda.tech.
